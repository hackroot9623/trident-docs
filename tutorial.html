Step-by-Step Instructions

Initializing a Group

  1. Start by creating a new Telegram Group.

  2. Proceed to open Telegram and add the bot to your newly created group.

  3. Upon addition, the bot will automatically introduce itself to the group and prompt for the name of the deal you wish to link with the group.

  4. Crucial Step! Ensure to promote the bot to an admin role for it to send messages correctly to HubSpot.

Linking a Deal with the Group

  1. The bot will request you to provide the name of the deal.

  2. Utilize the inline query to search for the deal by typing @your_bot_username deal_name in the group chat.

  3. Select the appropriate deal from the search results.

  4. The bot will then confirm the association and notify the group.

  5. To log a message, simply send a message to the group.

Troubleshooting Guide

  1. Confirm that the bot has the necessary permissions in the Telegram group.

  2. Inspect the logs for any error messages.
